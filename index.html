About

Face Aesthetics by Nichola

Advanced Aesthetic Clinic, Wirral

Welcome to Face Aesthetics by Nichola, where medical expertise meets artistic precision. Based in Wirral, our clinic is led by Nichola Colligan, an Independent Nurse Prescriber specializing in advanced non-surgical aesthetic treatments.

With a background in clinical nursing and specialized training in aesthetic medicine, we provide safe, evidence-based treatments that enhance your natural features while maintaining facial harmony and expression.

Our approach is patient-centered and holistic. Every treatment begins with a thorough consultation to understand your unique facial anatomy, concerns, and aesthetic goals. We believe in subtle enhancements that boost confidence and celebrate your individual beauty.
